Services

Who We Are

About

Purpose Built MFG started with a 3D printer, a garage, and the idea that we could build real things people actually needed — not just print for fun. Chasing that idea turned into our own business adventure — making useful parts for businesses around Jonesboro, AR. We have a variety of 3D printers capable of engineering-grade filaments — PC, PETG, ABS, carbon fiber composite, and more — and if your job calls for something specific, we'll work with you to source the right material and deliver a part that performs.

We work directly with HVAC contractors, auto shops, restaurant owners, and tradespeople across the region. If you've ever paid too much for a replacement part, waited weeks for something simple, or been told "we don't make that anymore" — that's exactly who we built this for.

Every job is quoted honestly, built to spec, and delivered fast. No sales process, no middle tier. You talk to the people who run the machines.
